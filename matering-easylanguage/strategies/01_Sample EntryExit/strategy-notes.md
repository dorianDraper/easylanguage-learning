## Notes
The default name of the strategy in the course is 'Sample EntryExit'. We rename it as 'EMA Trend Reversal-Cycle' since this is a descriptive name.

Each version incorporates improvements.

## Strategy Description
 Trend-following strategy based on a fast and slow exponential  moving average.

 Entries occur only in the direction of the current trend and only after the previous trade was in the opposite direction.
